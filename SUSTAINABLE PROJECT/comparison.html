AWS⭐⭐⭐
🎯 Net Zero: 2040 ♻️ 100% Renewable by 2025 💧 Water Positive Program
Amazon has committed to The Climate Pledge, targeting net-zero carbon by 2040. They have invested heavily in solar and wind energy across their data centers globally including India. However, due to their massive scale, their absolute carbon footprint is the highest.
Azure⭐⭐⭐⭐
🎯 Net Zero: 2030 ♻️ Carbon Negative by 2030 🕰️ Historic carbon removal by 2050
Microsoft has the most ambitious sustainability roadmap among the three. They plan to be carbon negative by 2030 and remove all the carbon they have ever emitted by 2050. Very strong commitment backed by actual investment in carbon capture technology.
GCP 🏆⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
🎯 Net Zero: 2030 ♻️ 24/7 Carbon-Free Energy 🌍 100% Renewable since 2017
Google is the most sustainable cloud provider. They have matched 100% renewable energy since 2017 and are working toward running on carbon-free energy 24/7 across all their data centers. Best choice for green computing and sustainable projects.
🏆
Final Recommendation
Which cloud should you pick?
AWS
Best overall ecosystem and support. Most services available. Highest cost. Best choice for large enterprises already using AWS tools and services.
Azure
Best for Microsoft and enterprise stack. Strong India presence with Pune and Chennai data centers. Good hybrid cloud support for existing on-premise apps.
GCP 🏆
Most cost-effective for Indian startups. Best sustainability record. Best for AI/ML workloads. Saves ₹1.1 lakh per month vs AWS for same workload.
